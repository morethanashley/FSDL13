How EcoRide Works

Join the electric vehicle sharing revolution in just a few simple steps

The EcoRide Process

From signing up to earning rewards, here's how you can make a difference

1

Create Your Profile

Sign up as either a driver or passenger. Complete your profile with verification to access all features. The process takes less than 5 minutes.

2

Plan Your Route

Enter your starting point and destination. Our algorithm will find matching rides or passengers along your route based on your preferences.

3

Connect & Ride

Review potential matches, communicate through our secure messaging, and confirm your ride. Track your trip in real-time with our app.

4

Earn Rewards

Accumulate carbon credits and EcoPoints for every shared mile. Redeem them for charging credits, cash rewards, or donate to environmental causes.
