Fruit List

  • Apple - A sweet red fruit that keeps doctors away!
  • Banana - A yellow fruit that's great for energy.
  • Orange - A citrus fruit packed with vitamin C.
  • Grapes - Small, juicy, and great for snacking.
  • Strawberry - A sweet red berry that's perfect for desserts.
  • Mango - A tropical fruit that's both sweet and sour.
